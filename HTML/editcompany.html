Introduction

Behn Meyer Vietnam offers a broad range of products for the rubber industry. Synthetic rubber such as SBR, NBR, and EPDM, as well as silicone rubber, are keys in the product portfolio. Behn Meyer is actively involved in the sales of curing agents e.g. accelerators as well as Nano CC filler. Rubber chemicals such as antioxidants, silanes, blowing agents as well as flame retardant are all provided by Behn Meyer and find their application in various industries. Release agents and processing aids such as lubricants, tackifiers, homogeniser, dispersing agents, peptizer and plasticiser are all produced by Performance Additives, Member of the Behn Meyer Group, and distributed by Behn Meyer’s sales team. A technically experienced team with excellent market knowledge is always at the customer’s disposal, with the aim to develop customer specific solutions and to strive for long-term cooperation.
